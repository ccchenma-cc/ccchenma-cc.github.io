Case study · AI Workflow GC

A repeatable AI content engine for marketing teams.

AI Workflow GC turns a marketing brief into researched, drafted, reviewed, and distribution-ready content through a controlled multi-step workflow.

Product lens

The workflow is designed for marketing teams that need consistent content output without losing brand judgment. It separates strategy, generation, review, and distribution so AI becomes a production system instead of a one-off drafting tool.

Project phases

01

Define the campaign brief

Clarify audience, channel, objective, offer, tone, constraints, and success metrics before any generation starts.

02

Research and angle selection

Collect competitive references, user pain points, keywords, objections, and hooks to form a strategic content direction.

03

Generate modular content

Produce outlines, posts, captions, email snippets, and variant copy from the same structured source of truth.

04

Review, score, and publish

Add human QA for brand voice, factual accuracy, platform fit, compliance, and measurable publishing readiness.

Workflow architecture

The system is organized as a chain of reusable stages: input brief, research brief, message angle, content draft, QA checklist, and distribution package. Each stage has a defined input and output, making the workflow repeatable across campaigns.

Execution notes

The key product decision was to keep human control at the moments that matter: choosing the strategic angle, approving claims, and finalizing brand voice. AI handles expansion, variation, and formatting, while the workflow preserves accountability.

Outcome

A professional marketing workflow prototype that demonstrates systems thinking, AI orchestration, content strategy, and the ability to convert an ambiguous business need into a scalable operating process.
